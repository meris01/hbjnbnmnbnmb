How to Get the Best Results

Be Specific with Topics

Instead of "Marketing", try "Content Marketing Strategies for SaaS startups". Clearer topics yield better results.

Use Relevant Keywords

Include primary and LSI keywords you want to rank for. Separate them with commas.

Define Your Audience

Telling the AI who the post is for (e.g., "beginners", "experts", "CEOs") helps tailor the language and depth.

Leverage the Brief

Use the optional brief for specific instructions, sections to include, points to emphasize, or things to avoid.

Edit & Refine

AI provides a great draft, but always review, edit, and add your unique voice and insights for the best final product.

Check SEO Elements

Ensure the generated post uses keywords naturally in headings (H2, H3) and throughout the text. Use the metadata as a guide.
